# Technical Indicators Reference

## Relative Strength Index (RSI)
- Standard Period: 14
- Overbought Threshold: > 70
- Oversold Threshold: < 30

## Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)
- Fast Length: 12
- Slow Length: 26
- Signal Length: 9

## Moving Averages
- Short Term (SMA/EMA): 9, 20
- Medium Term: 50
- Long Term: 200
